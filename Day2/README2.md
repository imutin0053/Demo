1. Largest of Three Numbers

Take three numbers as input and find the largest among them.

2. Leap Year Checker

Check whether a given year is a leap year or not.

3. Grade Calculator

Input marks of 5 subjects and calculate percentage.

A: ≥90
B: 80–89
C: 70–79
D: 60–69
F: <60
4. Positive, Negative, or Zero

Determine whether a number is positive, negative, or zero.

5. Even/Odd and Divisible by 5

Check whether a number is:

Even or Odd
Divisible by 5 or not
6. Valid Triangle Checker

Input three angles and check whether they can form a valid triangle.

7. Triangle Type Finder

Input three sides and determine whether the triangle is:

Equilateral
Isosceles
Scalene
8. Electricity Bill Calculator

Calculate electricity bill based on units consumed:

First 100 units → ₹5/unit
Next 100 units → ₹7/unit
Above 200 units → ₹10/unit
9. ATM Withdrawal System

Input balance and withdrawal amount.
Check:

Sufficient balance
Amount is multiple of 100
Minimum balance of ₹1000 remains
10. Number Classification

Check whether a number is:

Positive
Even
Divisible by both 3 and 5
11. Simple Calculator

Input two numbers and an operator (+, -, *, /, %).
Perform the corresponding operation.

12. Eligibility for Placement

A student is eligible if:

CGPA ≥ 7.0
No active backlogs
Attendance ≥ 75%

Determine eligibility.

13. Income Tax Calculator

Calculate tax based on annual income:

Up to ₹2.5 lakh → No tax
₹2.5–5 lakh → 5%
₹5–10 lakh → 20%
Above ₹10 lakh → 30%
14. Quadratic Equation Nature

Input a, b, c.
Find whether roots are:

Real and distinct
Real and equal
Imaginary

(Hint: Use discriminant b
2
−4ac)

15. Login Authentication System

Input username and password.
Check:

Username correct?
Password correct?
Both correct?
Display appropriate messages.
